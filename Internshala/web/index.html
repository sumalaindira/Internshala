Internship Day Registration

APSSDC along with Internshala is hosting an Internship Day in August. In this event, the colleges with highest internship selections from 1st April - 31st July 2018 will be rewarded. Internshala will provide all necessary support and guidance to the colleges to help place their students.

Important guidelines

  • Please upload the details of all the students enrolled in your colleges (1st -4th year).   Download sample file  
  • While uploading, please follow ensure that the excel sheet contains the following information of the students - Name, email address, phone number & Aadhar number.
  • Only internship selections from Internshala will be taken into account so please make sure all the student details are submitted. All these students will get an Internshala account through which they can apply to internships.
